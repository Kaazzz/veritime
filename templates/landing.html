Built for St. Catherine's College

Security and wellness,
side by side.

Veritime was designed with the Catherinean community in mind — giving faculty and staff the tools to ensure every student is safe, present, and accounted for.

Campus Security

Know exactly who has entered the campus in real time. Every card tap is logged with a timestamp, giving faculty an instant, accurate record of student arrivals.

Student Wellness Monitoring

Attendance patterns reveal a lot. Late arrivals and absences are tracked automatically, helping guidance staff identify students who may need support.

Live Dashboard

The moment a Catherinean taps their card, it appears on screen. Faculty see the student's name, photo, grade, section, and status — instantly, no refresh needed.

Records & Reporting

Filter attendance history by date, grade level, or section. Export any view as a clean CSV — ready for DepEd reporting, parent communication, or school records.

How It Works

Simple for staff.
Seamless for students.

01

Enroll Catherineans

Register each student with their full name, LRN, grade, section, and photo. Assign their RFID card by simply tapping it — no manual UID entry needed.

02

Set up the reader

Connect the Arduino RFID reader to the school's computer via USB. Veritime automatically detects it and starts listening — ready within seconds.

03

Monitor arrivals live

As students enter and tap their cards, the dashboard updates instantly — showing who arrived, when, and whether they were on time or late.
